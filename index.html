STRENGTH & WEEKNESS


If I had to explain what strengths and weaknesses I have, then I would say that the strength I have is reasoning or what could be called logical thinking. Every time I always think and ask questions about things that don't have clear answers, even when sleeping and in my dreams I still keep thinking about scenarios and solutions to an event.

As the saying goes, for every advantage there must be a disadvantage, so do I definitely have disadvantages. The weakness I have is that my energy quickly runs out when I am in a social environment for too long. When social matters are finished I will immediately go to a quiet place to restore energy.

My strengths and weaknesses complement each other. When I continuously think about finding solutions, my limited energy makes my brain work more relaxed so I can keep my thinking logical.
